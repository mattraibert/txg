Ingredients:
+ 3lbs Brussels Sprouts
+ OO
+ NaCl

Prep:
+ Cut B Sprouts in half
+ Toss with oil and salt
+ Spread on a baking sheet

Cooking:
+ 425 for 15-30 minutes. When they look like they're burning, they're ready.

Ingredients:
+ 2 pounds green beans
+ 1 pound mushrooms
+ 4 cloves garlic
+ salt and pepper

Prep:
+ trim green beans
+ mince 4 cloves garlic
+ break mushrooms into 1/2 inch pieces

Cooking:
+ fry garlic ginger for a minute
+ add mushrooms
+ blanch green beans in boiling water
+ drain
+ re-add green beans and stir fry a little more

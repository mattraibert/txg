Ingredients:
+ 2 cups sugar
+ 24 oz fresh cranberries
+ tsp orange marmelade
+ salt

Prep:
+ rinse berries

Cooking:
+ boil 2c water + 2c sugar + 1/4 tsp salt
+ stir in berries
+ boil at medium until saucy and 2/3 poppy
+ add marmelade to taste
+ cool
